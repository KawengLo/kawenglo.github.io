excavator control system

Interaction Design, HMI 2021

The project is based on the maturity of remote driving technology to envision new HMI system on the future XCMG machines including excavators, loaders and graders. The team cooperate with XCMG Machinery to unify and design the HMI system for future remote working environment

Being one of the leading construction machinery companies, XCMG Machinery wanted to step in the future of remote construction systems with the following three focuses and to integrate the interactions in three different machines

Compatibility
- Suitable for loaders, excavators and graders
Emotion
- To make the driver think less
Intuition
- To make boring driving fun
Challenge

How might we redesign the HMI of three machines of XCMG Machinery to provide a intuitive driving way in future remote working environment?

Ideation

Design focus 01

Unify the interaction of different construction machines, reducing training costs and simplifying and standardising driving behaviour

Design focus 02

Rationalised driving feedback to complement the driver's perception of the operating environment. Reduces the experience barrier, meets the driver's operational intuition and reduces driver physiological fatigue.

Design focus 03

Remote operation to solve safety problems, reduce fragmented working time and increase work efficiency.

Design Outcome

01 Interaction Paradigm Summary

Innovative interaction design

Operation control

Simplifies user input through an innovative approach to the digging process, where the user simply moves the operating control stick to adjust both the boom and arm, as there is a mechanical linkage behind that simulates a two-stage interaction of the machine

02 Interaction Paradigm Summary

① Using the terminal control approach

② With both fix length of boom and arm

③ To control the movement by stick

④ Reduced operational complexity
in a more intuitive way

Innovative interaction design

Movement control

Unifying the movement methods of different working machines including machines with track and wheel movements. The high degree moving freedom of the sphere allows for easier directional control and intuitive control. At the same time, the power can be adjusted by the knob behind.

02 Interaction Paradigm Summary

① Adjusting direction by pressing the button on the surface and turn the machine by moving the sphere

② Adjusting direction by pressing the button on the surface and turn the machine by moving the sphere
