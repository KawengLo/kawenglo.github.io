  01 Interaction Paradigm Summary

Innovative interaction design

Operation control

Simplifies user input through an innovative approach to the digging process, where the user simply moves the operating control stick to adjust both the boom and arm, as there is a mechanical linkage behind that simulates a two-stage interaction of the machine

02 Interaction Paradigm Summary

Innovative interaction design

Movement control

Unifying the movement methods of different working machines including machines with track and wheel movements. The high degree moving freedom of the sphere allows for easier directional control and intuitive control. At the same time, the power can be adjusted by the knob behind.
