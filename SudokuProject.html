UX UI, Game Design 2022

The App is dedicated to creating a popular science game for non-specialist users, utilising the mechanics of gamification design, incorporating gamification elements and the distillation of basic knowledge about calligraphy culture, attracting more people to learn about calligraphy culture by transferring active learning into passive absorption process through “Text-based Sudoku” gaming experience

Challenge

Why is there such a lack of general knowledge of Chinese calligraphy among public nowadays?

Research

Market Research, Competitive Research

The gap between
professional users and
unexperienced users

There is a gap between professional users and public. The main selling point of product for professional users is the completeness of the calligraphy inscriptions. The cost of learning to use the software is low for individuals to develop their calligraphic skills. However, it requires a strong self-driven approach for the users to use the app.

In other hand, the products for non-professional public users increase user engagement and interest through rich interactions, but this type of product is weak in guidance and difficult to popularise basic calligraphy knowledge

Therefore, the design value of the product is

How to raise the knowledge of public to calligraphy ?

How to integrated knowledge into more playful way so as to raise the public understanding of calligraphy?
