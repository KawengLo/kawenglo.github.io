Sudoku Game

UX UI, Game Design 2022

The App is dedicated to creating a popular science game for non-specialist users, utilising the mechanics of gamification design, incorporating gamification elements and the distillation of basic knowledge about calligraphy culture, attracting more people to learn about calligraphy culture by transferring active learning into passive absorption process through “Text-based Sudoku” gaming experience
