Mission

Information stucture

The first stage started with the development of design priorities with the product manager. The design objectives were developed taking into account the diversity of the service and the users. For the users which included government agencies, commercial users and individual users, as well as for the service includes the management, sale and use of copyright on the front and back ends. As a result, four main design goals were specified, including readability, uniformity, ease of use and extensibility

Building an Auto-layout Consoles

Grid System

In order to build a complex system, it is necessary to build a reasonable framework layout first. Therefore, the layout adopted the common left and right layout pattern, the left menu bar is fixed at 220px, using a two-level expandable navigation bar. The right content uses a grid system with 12 columns of adaptive grid so as to accommodate in different PC sizes
